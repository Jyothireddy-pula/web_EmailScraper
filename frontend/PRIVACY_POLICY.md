# Privacy Policy - AI Email Scraper

**Last Updated:** April 2026

## Information We Collect
- **Email Address**: For Google OAuth authentication
- **Email Content**: To provide AI-powered summaries and replies
- **API Keys**: Stored locally in your browser (not on our servers)

## How We Use Your Information
- **Authentication**: To connect to your Gmail account
- **Email Processing**: To generate summaries and auto-replies
- **AI Services**: To provide intelligent email analysis

## Data Security
- **No Server Storage**: We don't store your emails on our servers
- **Local Processing**: All processing happens in your browser
- **Secure OAuth**: Using Google's secure authentication
- **API Keys**: Encrypted and stored locally

## Third-Party Services
- **Google OAuth**: For secure Gmail access
- **Gemini API**: For AI-powered summaries (optional)
- **ChatGPT API**: For AI-powered replies (optional)

## Your Rights

- **Deletion**: You can delete your data anytime
- **Opt-out**: You can disconnect your Gmail account anytime
